Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Indira Love ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile dating application.

Personal Information:

Usage Information:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage patterns and interactions
  • Messages and chat history

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our dating services

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

5. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase secure cloud storage
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security audits

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control location sharing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings or by visiting our Account Deletion page. Upon deletion, your profile and personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.
