Delete Your Account

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read carefully before proceeding.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you delete your Indira Love account, the following will occur:

  • Your profile will be permanently removed from the app
  • All your photos and profile information will be deleted
  • Your matches and conversations will be deleted
  • Your likes and connections will be removed
  • You will lose access to any purchased subscriptions or premium features
  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Your profile becomes invisible to other users
  • Within 24 hours: All app data is removed from active servers
  • Within 30 days: All backup data is permanently deleted
Note: Some information may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records, abuse reports) as required by law.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Indira Love app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap Account Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your choice
  6. Enter your password to verify
  7. Tap Delete Permanently

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

Email: privacy@indiralove.app

Include in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Brief reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 5 business days.

Alternative: Temporarily Disable Your Account

If you're not ready to permanently delete your account, you can temporarily disable it:

  1. Go to Settings in the app
  2. Tap Account Settings
  3. Select Disable Account

Your profile will be hidden, but you can reactivate it anytime by logging back in.

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Disable notifications if you're getting too many
  • Temporarily disable your account for a break
  • Update your preferences to improve match quality
  • Contact our support team if you're having issues
